Common Mistakes

1. Trying to get through a whole lecture in one sitting
Many of these videos are not made with the student in mind. Being between 30 minutes to an hour in length, being able to use them effectively is crucial. They're often badly made, riddled with technical and other errors, and generally miss the point of a lecture and seek solely to introduce content.

If the lectures are recorded, try watching them in 30 minute chunks. If they're live, do your best to treat them like a normal lecture by turning off your phone and directing your attention solely towards the lecturer. Make sure to voice your concerns immediately and without hesitation. You're paying too much to be there, they can handle critisism.

Get a better alarm

Your alarm probably sucks. The stock iphone alarm has barely any customization and is overall lacking in any modern features. This is easily demonstrated. Get someone else to play your alarm. I bet you feel terrible. Now imagine how that affects your mental state every morning.

Get something like Alarmy on IOS and set the alarm to Random. You'd be amazed how big of a difference changing your alarm dynamic can make.
